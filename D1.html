Admission details


The basis of the program is modular training. Modular educational program of the specialty provides two paths (educational programs): 1. Accounting and auditing in the real sector of the economy. 2. Public sector accounting and auditing.

The main modules are the language and research module, the social and legal module, the module of modern information technologies, the basic economic module, the management module, the professional module. Training is conducted in three languages, we have also developed a multi-lingual group training.


Within the framework of the program, training courses are replaced by master classes, group projects and visits to companies. The emphasis is on practical training, allowing to develop the skills of analysis and audit using the data of specific economic entities, which meets the requirements of dual training.

Training of masters in this specialty is focused on training of qualified personnel who have deep knowledge in the field of accounting, applied financial and economic analysis and audit, the problems of adaptation of Kazakhstan accounting in accordance with international standards, the introduction of modern methods of management accounting in management, as well as knowledge in the field of higher education and psychology, teaching methods at the University, the skills of organizing and conducting research.
