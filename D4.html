Is your attention here
Specialty accountant -one of the most popular today professions in the field of Economics and Finance. Training of bachelors in the field of accounting and auditing is aimed at training qualified accountants and auditors who understand the concepts and specific rules of formation of information in accounting and reporting, possessing modern skills of accounting and auditing of financial statements.

Now functions of the accountant change and the range of the tasks facing it expands. From the counting technician engaged in registration of the facts of economic activity in accounts of accounting, the accountant turns into the creative personality, he provides formation and implementation of accounting policy of the organization necessary for effective management of economy of the organization.

Graduates of the specialty "Accounting and audit" can work as accountants, financial advisers, financial Directors, auditors, managers, financial analysts in Kazakh and international companies, in national and private companies, in the banking sector. A thorough knowledge of the rules of financial accounting helps in the management of their own business.
